A little about us...

This website is for anyone whom wants to practice their pronounciation.
We welcome you to try our Free Speaking feature which allows you to speak freely and we will tell you what we received!
You can also use the Speech game where we tell you what to say and you have to try and say the sentence/word.
Also, we record your tries and give you a score for your every try, and how will you know your overall score you ask....
That's what the Show My Statistics feature is for! I hope you find this website helpful and you recommend us to your friends!
